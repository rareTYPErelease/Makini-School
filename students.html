About Makini School


At The Makini School, our mission is to provide a nurturing and inclusive environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and a passion for lifelong learning. We envision a community where every student is empowered to reach their full potential, becoming responsible global citizens.

Our Students


Our students are at the heart of everything we do. We celebrate diversity and individuality, fostering a supportive community where each student feels valued and encouraged to express their unique talents.

Our Courses


Discover a diverse range of courses designed to cater to the interests and aspirations of our students. Our curriculum emphasizes a holistic approach to education, combining academic rigor with practical skills.

Our Sports


At The Makini School, we believe in the importance of a healthy mind and body. Our sports programs encourage teamwork, discipline, and a passion for physical well-being. Explore the exciting world of sports at our school!
